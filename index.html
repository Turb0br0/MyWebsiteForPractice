My Mission

Hello, and welcome to my list of open source apps that I want to make everyone aware of. It is important to me that the internet doesn't change for the worse and avoid coorporate domanance. I hope I can motivate you to contribute to open source and keep things free and available for everyone, if not then inspire you to create your own project and learn from it. Any small contribution to the internet that isn't a profit motivated company is a win for us all.

This video will cover some actions you can take to utilize open and free software. His name is Louis Rossmann in case you haven't heard of him. He has a bunch of videos explaining how the internet is changing. Also Notice the video is from Odysee, an American decentralized video hosting platform. A good alternative that isn't using you.

Odysee

App available for android and ios

Odysee is an American decentralized video hosting platform, built on the LBRY blockchain. It positions itself as an alternative to mainstream services like YouTube, but with a focus on freedom of speech and decentralization.
